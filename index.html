Frequently Asked Questions

1. What's the difference between lead generation and lead nurturing?

Lead generation brings in new contacts, while nurturing keeps them engaged and guides them toward a sale.

2. How does AI improve lead nurturing?

AI automates personalization, scoring, and timing-creating a tailored experience for every lead without manual effort.

3. Do AI SDR agents really work for nurturing?

Yes, they're trained to handle common queries, respond instantly, and nudge leads toward the next step.

4. Can I use AI automation with my existing CRM?

Absolutely. Most AI tools integrate with CRMs like HubSpot, Salesforce, or Pipedrive for seamless workflow automation.

5. Is AI nurturing suitable for B2B or B2C?

Both. B2B firms benefit from AI SDRs and long nurturing sequences, while B2C brands use AI for personalized product recommendations and retargeting.
